=== HOW TO PLAY ===

OBJECTIVE:
Survive waves of enemies and prevent them from reaching your base!

CONTROLS:
- ARROW KEYS: Move the cursor on the grid.
- ENTER: Place a tower at the cursor location.
- KEYS 1, 2, 3: Select Tower Type.

TOWER TYPES:
1. BASIC TOWER (White): Reliable single-target damage.
2. ICE TOWER (Cyan): Low damage, but SLOWS enemies down.
3. FIRE TOWER (Red): Burns enemies, dealing damage over time.

STRATEGY TIPS:
- Place Ice Towers at corners to maximize slow duration.
- Use Fire Towers early to whittle down high-health enemies.
